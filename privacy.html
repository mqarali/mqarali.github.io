Privacy Policy

Kalima Quest — Word Puzzle (com.kalimaquest.game)

Last updated: 27 June 2026

Kalima Quest is a free word-puzzle game developed by an independent developer in Jordan. We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information the app handles and why. By using Kalima Quest you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

Kalima Quest does not require an account and does not ask you for any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. Your game progress (levels completed, coins, streaks, settings) is stored only on your own device and is not transmitted to us.

Advertising

Kalima Quest displays ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google and its partners may collect and use certain information, including:

  • A device advertising identifier (Advertising ID) used to show relevant ads;
  • General, non-identifying device and usage information (such as device type, app version, and approximate region);
  • Interaction with ads (for example, whether an ad was viewed).

This data is handled by Google under its own privacy policy. Please review Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services. You can reset or limit your Advertising ID at any time in your device settings (Settings, Google, Ads).

Children

Kalima Quest is family-friendly and contains no violent or adult content. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads served in the app are configured for a general audience and are not behaviorally targeted to users under 13.

Data sharing and sale

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share personal data with third parties other than the advertising provider (Google AdMob) as described above, strictly to display ads within the app.

Your choices

  • Reset or delete your Advertising ID in your device settings.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored game data from your device.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
